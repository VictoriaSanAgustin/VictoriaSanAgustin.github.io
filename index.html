Introduction

Welcome to my data analytics portfolio adventure! After five years of navigating the wild jungles of parenthood, I decided to swap diapers for datasets, diving headfirst into the captivating world of data analytics. With no prior experience in this realm, but armed with an insatiable curiosity and a knack for crunching numbers honed through my accounting and buying escapades in both Namibia and the UAE, I embarked on a thrilling journey of discovery. Join me as I unravel the mysteries of data, one code at a time, with enthusiasm as my compass and spreadsheets as my trusty map!
